ABOUT US

CCET-ACM, one of the most reputed and distinguished technical chapter in CCET, we havw been working with vision and determination since it’s inception in 2015, with the aspirations of advancement of computer science as a science and a profession. We are not just an organization, we are a family, united by one cause, working towards making technology more accessible much like our parent organization, ACM.

CCET-ACM has been working on projects related to logo designing, web development, machine learning and app development and has been organizing events and workshop for the same.
